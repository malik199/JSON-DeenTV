Native Deen's DeenTV is the first 24/7 online entertainment channel for Muslims. DeenTV airs the most popular halal videos from around the world. DeenTV is currently producing reality shows, documentaries, comedy and music videos that are focused on stories of Muslims living in modern society. For more information or to get involved, got to our website at www.deen.tv

Our goals include:

  • Providing a 24/7 linear video channel with positive entertainment available worldwide via the internet.
  • Developing socially conscious programming acceptable by people of faith with the goal of creating a better world for humanity.
  • Creating a channel for professional, semi-professional and experienced amateur video producers to showcase their work on maintain belief in God while living in the West.
  • Allowing Muslim actors, journalists, writers, etc. to develop their talents in a wholesome manner.
